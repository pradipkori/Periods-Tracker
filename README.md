A premium, modern Flutter application designed for intuitive period tracking, ovulation prediction, and personalized health insights. Built with beauty and accuracy in mind.

## ‚ú® Features

- **Intuitive Tracking**: Log your period, symptoms, and moods with a sleek, glassmorphic interface.
- **Accurate Predictions**: Advanced logic that adapts to your unique cycle history, providing reliable next period and ovulation dates.
- **Dynamic Daily Insights**: Receive fresh, science-based health tips and advice tailored to your current cycle phase every single day.
- **Visual Analytics**: Beautiful charts and health scores to help you understand your body's patterns over time.
- **Privacy First**: Secure local storage using Isar database.
- **Export & Backup**: Generate PDF reports for your doctor or backup your data in JSON/CSV formats.

## üé® Design Philosophy

The app follows a **Rich & Dynamic Aesthetic**:
- **Phase-Based UI**: The app's color palette and background subtly shift based on your current cycle phase (Follicular, Ovulation, Luteal, or Menstrual).
- **Glassmorphism**: Modern frosted-glass components for a premium, lightweight feel.
- **Micro-animations**: Smooth transitions and interactive elements powered by `flutter_animate`.
